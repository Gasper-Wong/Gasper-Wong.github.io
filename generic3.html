About Me

I am Gasper, an aspiring data analyst with strong passion to help businesses succeed by making data-driven decision. I believe companies should leverage on the data available to understand more about their products and offerings to gain a competitive edge in the market.

In my previous role as an auditor, I have analysed reports generated using data analytics tool and derived relevant insights which helped to identify potential areas of concern for discussion with management. With a first hand experience on how the use of data can contribute to identifying trends, I decided to upskill my digital skills by attending the RISE program by Digital BCG Academy.

As a result, I have learnt important business skills and technical skills including Python, SQL and Power BI. I believe I would be able to apply my skills learnt to help businesses improve.
